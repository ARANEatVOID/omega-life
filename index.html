Mission Briefing

A Competition
Reborn

Impulse was always a spark. But this year, we've ignited the engine. For the first time, we're running this competition at full scale — bringing together the brightest minds from schools across the region for a day of pure technological reckoning.

Themed around Artificial Intelligence, Impulse 2025 asks one question: What happens when human creativity meets machine intelligence? Every event, every challenge, every moment has been designed around that collision.

This isn't just another fest. It's a statement. A signal. An impulse that we're sending into the future — and the future is transmitting back.
